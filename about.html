About Al-Ameen Engineering College

Al-Ameen Engineering College, located in Kulapully Shoranur, Palakkad, is a premier institution offering undergraduate courses in Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE), Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE), Mechanical Engineering (ME), and Civil Engineering (CE). The college is affiliated to Calicut University and approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).

The college was established in the year 2003 and since then it has been committed to providing quality education to the students. The faculty members are highly qualified and experienced and provide individual attention to each student to help them achieve their academic goals.

Courses Offered

  • B.Tech. in Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE)
  • B.Tech. in Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)
  • B.Tech. in Mechanical Engineering (ME)
  • B.Tech. in Civil Engineering (CE)

Facilities

  • Spacious classrooms and well-equipped labs
  • Library with a vast collection of books and online resources
  • Computer center with high-speed internet access
  • Transportation facility for the students and staff
  • Cafeteria serving hygienic and nutritious food
  • Indoor and outdoor sports facilities
  • Hostel accommodation for both boys and girls
